		<h2>The Midnight Shore, the Seas Beneath</h2>
		<p>I now stand on the dead, black carapace-shells of those inky-black shores. Under the Moon's light, the carapace-shells shine, like little jet-black Stars. Beyond lies an endless sea - stygian-black, like the uncharted fathoms of the heavens.</p>
		<p>As I look across the tar-black, inky sea, towards the horizon - I spot it, adrift on the sea: that dream-castle - where I must go, where I must end, where I must begin.</p>
